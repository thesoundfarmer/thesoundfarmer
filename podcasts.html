Greenwich Peninsular Ecology Park Audio Tour Sample

A promotional selection from the Greenwich Peninsular Ecology Park audio tour.

The extracts were chosen as they cover the various recording techniques used during the creation of the project, ranging from omni-pair, binaural stereo, parabolic and mono.

Best listened to with headphones.
